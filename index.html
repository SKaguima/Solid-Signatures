Solid Signatures is a global platform whose mission is to gather invaluable information and integrate it in a social medium to mentor and develop young professionals to become the next generation of leaders and entrepreneurs. With the current turmoil in the economy of many countries, employment has gone down, leaving many young people jobless, and even those who are lucky enough to get employment are in unsatisfactory positions that don't save them from escalating poverty. This can be reversed with the engagement into entrepreneurial activities that lead to self-employment and development in leadership skills so that young people can be molded into strong pillars for the society.
